My Experience as a Judge

I am pleased to share my experience as a judge at the Alchemist's Treasure Hunt, a chemistry investigation module at STEM FEST 3.0, held at Cambridge Campus Gulshan on November 9, 2023.

During this event, I evaluated students' performances in hands-on tasks that tested their understanding of chemical concepts, analytical skills, and critical thinking. My role involved providing constructive feedback and fostering an engaging learning environment for the participants. This experience allowed me to appreciate the importance of nurturing young talent in STEM and enhanced my skills in assessing student understanding and delivering effective feedback.
